Privacy Policy for Invoice (com.atcorp.invoice)

Last updated: 27 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Invoice app ("we", "us", "the app") handles information when you use it.

1. Data we collect

The Invoice app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely offline.

2. Data stored on your device

The app stores the following information locally on your device, only for the app's own use:

  • Your business profile (name, address, GSTIN, bank details, tax defaults) — stored in the app's private preferences.
  • The invoices you create (party details, line items, totals) — stored in a local SQLite database within the app's private storage.

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Permissions

The app requests no special permissions. PDF generation, printing, and sharing are handled through the standard Android share sheet, which prompts you each time.

4. Third-party services

The app uses no analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services.

5. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13.

6. Data deletion

All app data lives within the app's storage on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all of it. There is nothing for us to delete because we never receive any of your data.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any change.
